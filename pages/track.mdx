# How can I track my games and tournaments?

The
[cEDH Metagame Project](https://www.reddit.com/r/CompetitiveEDH/comments/udj37u/cedh_metagame_project_version_5_2_month_update/)
is a survey of self-reported cEDH games, and
[budgetbrews.club](https://budgetbrews.club/deck-lists/#finals) keeps a list of
finalists in cEDH tournaments. If you'd like to help us track changes to the
cEDH metagame across the world, report your games and/or tournament finalists
below.
